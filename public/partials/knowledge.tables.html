Basic Table

Code:
<table class="table">
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <td>Title A</td>
      <td>Title B</td>
      <td>Title C</td>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td>Data 1</td>
      <td>Data 2</td>
      <td>Data 3</td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>Data 4</td>
      <td>Data 5</td>
      <td>Data 6</td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>
